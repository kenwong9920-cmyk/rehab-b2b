Toilet Safety Rails & Risers — Complete B2B Product Line

Toilet safety products serve a seemingly simple function — helping users sit down and stand up safely — but the engineering requirements vary enormously depending on user needs, bathroom layout, and installation conditions. Our product line addresses this diversity through two product families: toilet safety rails (floor-standing frame type) and toilet risers (toilet-mounted type).

### Toilet Safety Rails: The Frame Family

Our safety rail line encompasses over 80 SKU variants, yet they share a single design philosophy: clamp-on installation — no drilling, no wall damage, no specialized tools required. The core variables that differentiate models are:

**Frame Material:** High-carbon steel (standard, most models) or aluminum alloy (lighter weight, select models). Both come standard in white powder coat with custom colors available.

**Clamp Configuration:** Twin-clamp (two locking points) or quad-clamp (four locking points — significantly more stable). Fixed clamps hold the set position; movable clamps allow the frame to slide forward and back for flexible positioning before locking.

**Armrest Type:** Standard fixed armrests or independently height-adjustable wide armrests. Wide armrests provide a broader contact surface — the user's entire forearm can rest flat, reducing pressure concentration and fatigue even during extended toilet use.

**Foot Tip Type:** Threaded tips (adjustable height, ideal for uneven floors) or suction cup tips (excellent grip on smooth surfaces, but require a clean, level floor).

**Armrest Sleeve Color:** Black, grey, or dark grey soft-touch foam.

A reinforcement strap connects the frame to the toilet base for additional stability — a small detail that eliminates the micro-wobble that develops over time in cheaper frames.

Our cross-border stainless steel rail series features a single flat-tube front bar with threaded foot tips and foam-sleeved armrests. Three powder-coated finish options are available — cream white matte, Tiffany green, and matte black — color choices informed by Amazon category analysis showing that non-white options generate higher click-through rates in the bathroom safety category.

### Toilet Risers: The Riser Family

Our toilet riser series (LM112/113) addresses the combined need for height assistance and lateral support. Key features:

**LM112 Series (High-Carbon Steel):** Fixed dual-clamp installation with wide armrests. The armrest width is sufficient for the user's entire forearm to rest flat — a distinguishing feature that sets it apart from competitors using narrow-tube armrests. Seat cushion: U-shaped leather. Available with or without commode bucket. Threaded foot tips standard, suction cup tips optional.

**LM113 Series (Aluminum Alloy/Stainless Steel):** Upgraded materials with foam-sleeved armrests for more comfortable grip. The clamp mechanism supports stepless adjustment front-to-back and side-to-side — users can fine-tune frame positioning without tools. The carbon fiber leg tube variant (LM113T) combines an aluminum alloy main frame with carbon fiber leg tubes for reduced weight.

The clamp design uses engineered plastic contact surfaces rather than exposed metal screws — an important detail, since exposed screw heads can scrape the user's legs during transfers.

### FAQ

Will these rails fit my toilet?

Our standard rails accommodate toilet widths of 35–48 cm. Adjustable-clamp models cover an even wider range. For non-standard toilets, please contact us with your toilet dimensions.

Is installation reversible? Does removal leave any marks?

Yes. The clamp design leaves no marks on the toilet or floor. This is particularly important for rental properties and care facilities that may need to reconfigure rooms.

Do you offer wall-mounted grab bars?

Our current product line focuses on floor-standing toilet frames. Wall-mounted grab bars are under development.
