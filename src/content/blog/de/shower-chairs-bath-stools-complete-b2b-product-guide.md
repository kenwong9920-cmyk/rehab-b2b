Shower Chairs & Bath Stools — Complete B2B Product Guide

A shower chair endures the harshest conditions of any bathroom product: constant moisture, temperature fluctuations, exposure to cleaning chemicals, and use by individuals with compromised balance. For B2B buyers, the product characteristics that separate a shelf-worthy chair from one that generates returns are nearly invisible in catalog photos — but they become unmistakably clear within the first week of use.

### Classic Bath Stools & Shower Chairs: The Core Product Line

Our classic shower seating range (LM5100/5101/3200/3202 series) features aluminum alloy main frames with white or wood-grain finish. The most fundamental distinctions within this range are stool (seat only) versus chair (seat + backrest), and fixed height versus adjustable height.

Adjustable-height models offer 6-level height adjustment via push-button spring pins. Foot tip options include standard non-slip ribbed tips, suction cup tips (providing extra grip on smooth surfaces), and angled tips (which outperform flat tips on wet, potentially soapy surfaces). All seated models include an EVA cold-proof cushion, eliminating the jarring shock of sitting on cold metal or plastic during winter bathroom use.

For users who need arm support during transfers, the LM3200/3202 series adds foam armrests — a highly practical feature for elderly users who rely on armrests to sit down and push themselves up. The LM5104 series adds a removable commode pan, transforming the shower chair into a shower-toilet combination chair.

### Swivel Seat Bath Stools: Innovation Driven by Word of Mouth

Our swivel bath stools feature a sealed bearing mechanism, enabling 360-degree seat rotation. For users with limited upper body mobility, being able to rotate while seated — rather than twisting the torso — is a genuine safety improvement. Features like this naturally generate word-of-mouth momentum within caregiver and occupational therapist communities.

### FAQ

**Q: What's the difference between threaded tips and suction cup tips?**

A: Threaded tips allow fine height adjustment by screwing in or out. Suction cup tips grip more securely on smooth surfaces but require a clean, flat floor to adhere effectively. For textured or uneven bathroom floors, threaded tips are more reliable.

**Q: Are your shower chairs rust-proof?**

A: Aluminum alloy main frames are inherently corrosion-resistant and will not rust. Properly powder-coated stainless steel frames are equally rust-proof. We subject our coated steel frames to 48-hour salt spray testing with zero corrosion points.

**Q: What certifications do your shower chairs carry?**

A: CE certification covers the entire range. ISO 13485 covers our manufacturing facility. Select models hold additional certifications — please contact us with your market access requirements.
